# MAHADUM.360 Landing Art Direction

## Purpose

This document is the visual continuity guide for the landing-page image system. It supplements the master product and brand guidance; it does not replace the canonical wordmark or tagline.

## Character Bible

### Iya

- Nigerian grandmother, approximately 68.
- Warm round face, medium-dark brown skin, expressive smile lines.
- Navy-and-orange geometric gele, indigo blouse, small round gold earrings.
- Calm, witty, emotionally present; never presented as a costume or historical prop.

### Amara

- Nigerian granddaughter, approximately 8.
- Medium-dark brown skin, two puff braids with orange bands.
- Yellow T-shirt and denim overalls.
- Curious, confident, visibly delighted by speaking and understanding.

Keep face shape, hair, clothing, proportions, and age consistent across scenes. Secondary children and adults can change with context, but Iya and Amara anchor the system.

## Illustration Style

- Premium contemporary 3D/2.5D children’s editorial illustration.
- Soft rounded forms, expressive faces, tactile materials, clean silhouettes.
- Bright cobalt and sky blue, energetic orange, warm yellow, white, and deep navy.
- Natural Nigerian homes, courtyards, classrooms, objects, and clothing without generic “African” symbolism.
- No visible text, logos, watermarks, UI lettering, flags, safari motifs, or costume-like pattern overload.
- Warm directional light and enough negative space for responsive copy overlays where requested.

## Produced Scene Set

1. **Hero — grandmother and child learning**

   Iya and Amara laugh together over a tablet in a bright contemporary Nigerian living room. The emotional focus is recognition and connection, not the device.

2. **Culture — courtyard storytelling**

   Iya, Amara, and two cousins share a folktale around a lantern at blue hour in a contemporary Nigerian courtyard. The scene should feel magical because of attention and imagination, not fantasy clichés.

3. **Schools — collaborative classroom**

   Amara and two classmates practise together with a warm Nigerian teacher using a shared tablet and notebooks in a bright modern classroom.

4. **Social cover — wide family connection**

   Iya and Amara occupy the right side of a wide pale-blue composition, leaving quiet space on the left for platform-generated social copy.
